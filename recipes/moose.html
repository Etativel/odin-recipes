Chocolate Moose

What's the plural of moose?... meese :D

Chocolate mousse is a classic and elegant dessert that's sure to satisfy any chocolate lover's cravings. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces (170 grams) of high-quality dark chocolate (70% cocoa or higher)
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup (50 grams) granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup (240 ml) heavy cream
  • A pinch of salt

Instruction

  1. Start by melting the dark chocolate.
  2. Separate the egg yolks from the egg whites.
  3. Add half of the granulated sugar (1/8 cup or 25 grams) to the egg yolks and beat them together.
  4. Combine the egg yolks with melted chocolate
  5. In a separate clean bowl, beat the egg whites with a pinch of salt until they form stiff peaks.
  6. In another bowl, whip the heavy cream until it forms soft peaks.
  7. Carefully fold the whipped egg whites into the chocolate mixture in three additions.
  8. Spoon the chocolate mousse into serving dishes or glasses and refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours, or until it's set. Enjoy :)
